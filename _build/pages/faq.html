How long is the program?

12 weeks.

Who will you be working with?

Your homebase will be The Art Department. The Art Department is Tumblr’s in-house creative team. We collaborate with every other team within the company, providing design expertise across a variety of mediums.

We work with these teams: Product Design, Product Engineering, Marketing, Events, Music, Fashion, Social

On these kinds of projects:
Illustrations and animations Printed materials and wearable items Typography, layout, logo and brand development Photography and video direction.

What is the structure?

If selected, your time will be divided between working with The Art Department, and developing a self-initiated project.

What is the self-initiated project?

The self-initiated project should:
(a) Leverage or improve Tumblr’s existing product (iOS/Android app, website)
and/or
(b) Utilize, examine, or benefit the Tumblr community.

What's the outcome?

While the guidelines are intentionally open, the expectation is that your self-initiated project will resolve in a tangible form.

What compensation is offered?

$25,000, over 12 weeks.
Payment is “All-in”. Secondary compensation is not offered for materials or rent, unless agreed upon on an individual basis.

What materials are provided?

Individual accommodations will be decided based on the selection of participants. Participants should expect to be provided with standard equipment, including:
A laptop
Desk space
Access to office amenities

What's the housing situation?

During the time of the residency, artists are expected to work out of our New York City office.

The payment of $25,000 operates like traditional income — it can be used for any expenses the artist see fit, including rent. Additional compensation for housing is not offered as a separate expense.
