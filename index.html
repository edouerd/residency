You are here attempts to straddle the line between an artist residency and a full-time freelance position.

Tumblr is founded on the promise of limitless expression. Do, act, say, how you feel. We produce the tools and a stage, and our community uses them to spread their message to the world.

You are here explores a new way to build more tools, and more stages, tailored to you.

We have a small staff of full-time designers and writers, but we only represent the smallest fraction of the creativity and talent on Tumblr.

From Activists and organizers to photographers and 3D specialists, You are here hopes to work with the the most thoughtful voices in the community.
